Pan Am Championship 2016


Great news from Irvine California!!!

Brazil-021 Houston had 2 athletes representing our team, Fernando Aramburo (Blue belt Pan Am Champion 2015), and Danny Kou (Blue Belt Pan Am Champion 2015).

Both, Fernando and Danny were there to defend their titles and even though Fernando is recuperating from a knee surgery, he madesure to do his best to be ready to represent our team and fight. Even losing by 1 advantage on his first fight, Fernando showed that he is back to the competition scenery and it is just a matter of time until he starts winning titles like in 2015 again.

Danny got his purple belt 4 months ago, and already have won Houston Open and completely dominated the light weight division to become the 2016 Pan Am light weight Champion (master 1 division). With a flawless performance, Danny also fought the absolute and lost by 2 points at the final match, bringing two medals back to Houston!

Congratulations to Fernando, Danny and the whole Brazil-021 family members who helped our athletes during the preparation and training. Their victory is also yours!!!!”

 

 

 

July 2015 British National (GI / NO GI)


Professor Marcos Cerqueira wins the British National Absolute

On June 27th and 28th the first edition of the British National Championship, organized by IBJJF (International Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Federation) and UKBJJF (United Kingdom Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Federation) took place in London, United Kingdom. Brazil-021 International was represented by its athletes, Marcos Cerqueira (Black Belt) and Danny Kou (Blue Belt). Professor Marcos Cerqueira was coaching his athlete Danny Kou (Pan American Champion 2015 and European NO GI Champion 2015) and fighting alongside Danny for weight division and absolute. It was an awesome trip and our team brought home a total of 5 medals, 3 gold, 1 silver and 1 bronze!

Congratulations to not only Professor Marcos Cerqueira and Danny Kou, but also to Jaileen Chang and Lim Kim who were there in person to support our athletes, and to the whole Brazil-021 Family for helping during training! Let’s keep training hard for our next tournament! OSS

"Unidos somos mais fortes"... Together we are stronger!

 

 

 

 

 

April 2015 European NO GI / ROME OPEN


VENI, VIDI, VICI! Great Results for our team at European NO GI and ROME OPEN 2015

On April 10th, 11th and 12th, Three athletes from our team went to Rome (Italy) to compete at the European NO GI and Rome Open Tournament. Marcos Cerqueira (Black Belt) got 2nd at the Rome Open, Bailey Gao (Blue Belt) won his Division taking the Gold Medal home and also took 3rd place in the absolute division. Danny Kou (Blue Belt) took Gold on his divison and became the 2015 NO GI European Champion and got 3rd in the absolute division!

Thank you to each member of this family for helping everybody who fought during their preparation training as well. Without you this could not be possible. We got great results but we are going to keep working hard to improve always!

"Unidos somos mais fortes"... Together we are stronger!
