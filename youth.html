Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu has no age restriction, and for kids it is a very fun way to exercise, increase motor skills, flexibility and more.

The child who practices jiu-jitsu grows up confident, healthy and learns from an early age how to handle any kind of situation.

Our Youth BJJ Program was recently showcased on a KHOU Channel 11 Houston news segment regarding the growing number of parents that have turned to new tactics to protect their children from bullies.


NEW FAMILY CLASS


Saturdays 9:00 a.m. - 10:00 a.m.
There’s no better way to start off your weekend, than spending time with you children on the mats!
This class is for parents who would like to spend time with their kids on the mats. The idea is to have fun to have and a great bonding moment with their loved ones while leaning Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and Self-Defense together. Only parents fathers and mothers or legal guardians will be allowed in the class. No child can train alone, and no parent can be on the mat without his or her children for this class.
